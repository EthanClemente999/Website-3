WHY do we participate in sports?

Sports have a profound impact on our lives. They strengthen our social connections and improve both our mental and physical health. Moreover, sports teach us resilienceâ€”pushing us to keep striving despite the challenges and pain we must endure to meet even the basic demands of the game. A prime example is Formula 1 or also known as F1. The sport is incredibly demanding, physically and mentally, with reports stating that the average person cannot drive an F1 car. It requires immense understanding, financial investment, time, effort, teamwork, knowledge of aerodynamics, exceptional driving skills, and the ability to endure high G-forces. On top of that, there's the inherent risk of severe injury or even death in crashes, given the extreme speeds these cars can reach (233 mph or 374.97 km/h).
